Join A Department

Welcome to DC Prime! You can now join and register with a department based on your interests and skills. Make the most out of hands-on training and experience serving in a department and help us bring order to the Lord's house.

We shelter various departments that suit different interests and that ignite your creativity: from our superb youth choir, to our ushers, the media team, creativity team, MVPs, Protocols and so much more. Your departmental administrator will reach out to you!


Volunteer With Us Through the Following Ministries

Ushering & Protocol.

Join our ushering & protocol team and be the first point of contact for our members and guests! As an usher, you'll create a warm and welcoming environment, ensuring everyone feels valued and supported.

Intercessory Team

Be a part of the intercessory team, let's use prayer to make a difference. Your commitment to prayer will be a vital support to our ministry.

Music Team.

Whether you sing or play an instrument you'll be part of creating an atmosphere of praise and worship. Join us in leading our people in inspiring music sessions.

Creativity Team

Express your creativity and help us communicate the message of God's love! Our creativity team uses art, design, dance, drama and lots more to inspire and uplift our community. Join us in using your talents to make a difference.

Administrative Support.

Use your organizational skills to support our ministry! as an administrator, you'll help with tasks such as planning, coordination and communication. Your attention to detail and ability to multitask will ensure our community runs smoothly.

Content & Media Team

Help us share our message of God's love through various media channels! We are building and raising excellent content creators. Our content team creates content for social media, websites and other platforms. If you have writing, photography or videography skills, join us in spreading the word!

Technical & Instrumentalists.

Bring your technical expertise to our technical, sound and Instrumentalists team. Join us in creating a professional atmosphere for our services and events.

MVP Team

Show God's love and care to our community by joining our MVP (Most valued persons) or welfare team! You will provide support and assistance to those in need, whether it's practical help or a listening ear. Your compassion and empathy will make a significant impact on people's lives.
