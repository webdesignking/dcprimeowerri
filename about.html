We Are Dominion City Prime

We are passionate about creating a safe and inspiring environment where young people can grow in their faith, discover pupose and make global impact. To effectively cater to the unique needs of our members, we are divided into two distinct groups: DC Prime, designed for teens aged 12-16, and DC Prime Transition tailored for youths aged 17-25.qa

Our Purpose.

To inspire a generation of young people to become passionate followers of Jesus Christ. To empower teens and youths to discover their God-given purpose and potential, fostering a community of belonging, support and encouragement. To develop leaders who will shape their world with integrity, compassions and wisdom partnering with families and the wider church to promote spiritual growth and maturity.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to raise leaders that transform society... igniting a passion for God and his purpose in the hearts of young people, equipping them to become influential leaders, passionate worshippers and compassionate change-makers in their communities and beyond. By living out this mission, we aim to transform lives, shape culture and bring glory to God!
